About us

We are a non-profit animal rescue organization. Based in Pleven, Bulgaria, we've been rescuing animals for a few years now and have found loving homes in Bulgaria, Austria, Germany and Switzerland for over 50 dogs and cats.

We're currently in the process of building a shelter. Unfortunately, until it's completed, our limited space makes it even more urgent to find homes for these angels.
